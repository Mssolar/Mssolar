Solar PV Knowledge Base

Understand the basics of how solar photovoltaic (PV) systems work to generate clean, renewable energy for your home or business in Rajasthan.

A solar PV system, or solar power system, is composed of high-efficiency solar panels (modules), a robust mounting system, and a smart inverter with a computerized controller. Our solar panels are made of silicon, which acts as a semiconductor. When sunlight (photons) from the Rajasthan sun hits the silicon, it excites electrons, creating a direct current (DC) of electricity. This is known as the photovoltaic effect, the core of solar energy generation.

The DC electricity then flows from the panels to an inverter. The inverter's crucial job is to convert the DC electricity into alternating current (AC) electricity. AC is the standard form of electricity used to power appliances in our homes and businesses. This usable AC power then flows from the inverter to your electrical panel (breaker box) and is distributed throughout your property, reducing your reliance on the grid and lowering your electricity bills.
