Our Product Range

Discover the high-quality solar products we offer to power your sustainable future.

Solar Panels

We offer a variety of solar panels to suit different needs and budgets, ensuring maximum energy capture and durability.

  • Monocrystalline Panels: High efficiency, sleek design, ideal for limited space.
  • Polycrystalline Panels: Cost-effective, good performance in standard conditions.
  • Thin-Film Panels: Flexible, lightweight, suitable for diverse applications.
  • Bifacial Panels: Generate power from both sides, increasing overall yield.

Inverters

The brain of your solar system, converting DC electricity from panels into usable AC electricity for your home or business.

  • String Inverters: Cost-effective for most residential and small commercial systems.
  • Micro-inverters: Maximize individual panel output, ideal for shaded or complex roofs.
  • Hybrid Inverters: Combine solar inversion with battery storage management.
  • Off-Grid Inverters: Essential for standalone solar systems without grid connection.

Mounting Structures

Robust and durable systems to securely install your solar panels, designed for various roof types and ground installations.

  • Rooftop Mounts: Pitched and flat roof solutions, non-penetrating options available.
  • Ground Mounts: Freestanding structures for open land, adjustable for optimal tilt.
  • Carport Structures: Dual-purpose structures providing shade and generating power.
  • Custom Solutions: Engineered designs for unique architectural requirements.

Solar Batteries

Store excess solar energy for use during the night or power outages, ensuring energy independence.

  • Lithium-ion Batteries: High energy density, long lifespan, popular for residential use.
  • Lead-Acid Batteries: Traditional, cost-effective option for larger systems.
  • Flow Batteries: Scalable, long-duration storage for commercial and industrial applications.
  • Battery Management Systems (BMS): Optimize battery performance and longevity.

Accessories & Monitoring

Complete your solar system with essential accessories and advanced monitoring tools.

  • Cables and Connectors: High-quality, UV-resistant wiring for safe and efficient power transfer.
  • Charge Controllers: Regulate voltage and current from solar panels to batteries.
  • Monitoring Systems: Real-time performance tracking via web or mobile apps.
  • Safety Equipment: Fuses, circuit breakers, and rapid shutdown devices.
